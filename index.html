PIG DICE GAME INSTRUCTIONS

  • Players will take turns to roll the die.
  • You can only stop rolling if you click the 'hold' button.
  • Your score for a particular round will go to 0 if the die lands on 1.
  • This is a cue for player 2 to start playing
  • The game ends when one of the players gets to 100 points first.
